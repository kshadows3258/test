Safety Tips For a Happy Halloween

Tip 1 Tip 2 Tip 3 Tip 4 Tip 5

Safety Tip 1:

Never allow young children to trick-or-treat alone. Young children should always be accompanied by an adult or an older sibling when trick-or-treating, especially after dark.
back to top

 

Safety Tip 2:

Always check childrens' candy before they are allowed to eat it. Remove and throw out anything without a wrapper or with a wrapper that is damaged. Make sure children know not to eat any candy they receive until it has been checked by an adult.
back to top

 

Safety Tip 3:

No matter what costume a child is wearing, ensure they have reflective tape, a glow stick or a flashlight to make them visible to other trick-or-treaters and automobiles.
back to top

 

Safety Tip 4:

Before allowing children out to trick-or-treat, go over basic safety rules. Children should know not to get into automobiles or enter homes of anyone while they are out collecting candy.
back to top

 

Safety Tip 5:

If older children are trick-or-treating without a parent or adult, set a designated time to check in or be home. Make sure to plan out a route ahead of time so they know where they are allowed to go.
back to top
